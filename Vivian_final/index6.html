Hanzhong Road Station

Line 1,12,13

"If the first thing that comes into my mind in the morning is sunshine, the thing that follows next is that it's hatching butterflies." - Nabokov This is a new media lighting installation called "underground butterfly magic forest", located at Hanzhong Road Station. This work is insipied by Chinese philosophy Chuang Chou's dream of becoming a butterfly. Combined with 3D printing technology, every butterfly is delicate and vivid, showing the ingenious combination of oriental philosophy and western technology.
